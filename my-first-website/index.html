Sylvan

Scientific Name:

Sylvan

Discovered by / at:

Discovered by Sara He in the Enchanted Grove region in 2024

Family:

Sylvan and her family once lived happily together in the forest. However, the forest was degraded, and they lost their living conditions, with many of them died. Sylvan is the only survivor.

Habitat:

Sylvan lives in an ancient forest with rich biodiversity. However, the forest has been devastated. Sylvan and her family lost their habitat and she hopes to rebuild her home.

Appearance:

Initially, Sylvan appears as a small, translucent being. As she chooses to return items to the forest, she regains her memory. She realized that her body can undergo a transformation, gradually turning green in the sunshine. In her final form, she resembles a vibrant and energetic elf, with pointed ears and ethereal green hues.

Key Characteristics:

  • Has the ability to change its transparency in the sunshine
  • Grows in size as she collects forest-related items
  • Can leave behind footprints of various shapes when walking along the road

What do we know so far about the creature?

Sylvan is a survivor of the elf species, which once thrived in the forest it woke up in. The elf species faced extinction due to forest destruction, and the creature lost its memory during this time. Sylvan is motivated by a deep connection to its forest habitat and a desire to restore the natural balance that was disrupted by the destruction of the elf species' home.
